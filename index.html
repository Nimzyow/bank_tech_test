To run this amazing application, please open up your chrome browser and type the following:

To create an account called account, type:

let account = new Account()

To make a deposit of 1000 into your account, called account, type:

account.deposit(1000)

To withdraw 500 from your account, type:

account.withdraw(500)

You can deposit and withdraw however much you like but if you try to withdraw more than what you have, you'll get a big fat warning so be careful!!

to view a print out of your statement, simply type:

account.statement()
